How the show works

It plays like darts, thinks like a survey, and feels like a game show. Every team starts at 101.

1

Guess the top answer

We surveyed real people. Your team calls out what they think the most popular answer is — no obscure facts, just crowd smarts.

2

Take it or press your luck

Bank your points, or gamble on the second and third most popular answers for a double or triple. Greed is encouraged.

3

Land on exactly zero

Points count you down from 101. Overshoot and you bust. First team to hit zero on the nose wins the night.
