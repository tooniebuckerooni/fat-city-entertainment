How To Print Bingo Cards Using Random Generator

Instructions:
  1. Create a title for your game.
  2. Input minimum 25 answers using a comma (,) to separate answers.
  3. Add a hyphen and space (- ) to long words to break them up.
  4. Choose if you want a random or centered 'Free Space' and what to call it.
  5. Add number of cards to create.
  6. Click Download PDF to generate and save file.
  7. Print on colored card stock paper for best results.
  8. Cut out cards (Optional)
