Virtual Events: The Safe Choice Of Hosting Your Next Function Online

We have 3 main game options to choose from that work well with online virtual events, but we are open to your ideas and are always welcome to 'custom' events.

Music Bingo
Fun and not-too-tough music based game where players mark off answers on bingo cards, trying to make a line, 4 corners, or Blackout! for prizes. Over 45 different music bingo game genres to choose from. Think about playing 1-3 at your event for a casual game that entices people to continue talking, as well.

Fat Bottom Trivia - "Game Show" Trivia 
This is is a more traditional style of trivia with categories on the big-screen for guests to choose from. There are 5 categories of questions with 3 hidden Side-Games and a unique Final Challenge for each game genres. Choose your genre from: General Knowledge, Music, or Sports.

Triv101 - "Survey Style" Trivia
Using a classic dart board layout, teams start with 101 points and have to guess their way down to exactly Zero by answering popular choice questions.
