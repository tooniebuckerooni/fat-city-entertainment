Music Bingo
Fun and not-too-tough music based game, where players "Dob" off correct songs to make a Bingo! on their cards. 35 Games to choose from.

Music Bingo, sometimes called Mingo, is a fun and casual game very popular right now. Hosts pass out cards to guests and take them through fun musical journeys. When you recognize the song, you "dob" off the correct square. We often give prizes for the 1st Line, an X or Plus Sign, or a Blackout. Try something new, like a letter of the alphabet or a hashtag!
