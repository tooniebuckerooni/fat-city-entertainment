Migrate fatcityentertainment.com from Weebly to static GitHub Pages site

Every public URL is preserved: 32 exported pages, 73 product pages
(/store/pNN/, incl. 2 hidden products), 8 store categories, 107 blog
posts plus archives/categories/pagination (extensionless URLs served
as directory index.html).

- Weebly platform JS/CSS removed; CDN assets (sites.css, fonts, icon
  fonts, jQuery, slideshow) self-hosted under /assets/
- Product Add-to-Cart replaced with LemonSqueezy buy buttons driven
  by assets/js/ls-links.js (see LEMONSQUEEZY-TODO.md for the 73-item
  re-listing checklist)
- Weebly video embeds converted to native video tags (files in /uploads)
- Cloudflare email obfuscation decoded back to real mailto: links
- Legacy URLs (/4/post/, /blog/, /inspiration/, old store slugs,
  renamed pages) rewritten to match the live site's redirect behavior
- Booking forms point at a Formspree placeholder (YOUR_FORM_ID)
- 519 Weebly-generated image derivatives fetched from the live site
- CNAME, robots.txt, sitemap.xml (original 213 URLs), 404 page
- _tools/ holds the scraper/transformer pipeline and raw scraped pages
