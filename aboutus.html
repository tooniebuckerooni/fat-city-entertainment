Our Mission: Who we are, and why you should want to work with us.
(tl;dr -> We’re Awesome Entertainers.)

We are a society of Entertainers who love bringing people together. Believe it or not, we even have morals and scruples too. As Entertainers, we have an obligation to unite audiences and we break down prejudices (I really wanted to leave untie here as an auto-correct). We really are trying to make the world a better place the best way we know how... With a microphone.

We have lots of fun projects going on, one of which I am very excited about is our Safe Driving Initiative in Charlotte NC! However, we are not limited to any one place. For a reasonable price, we travel to you! But most importantly, our games are accessible anywhere in the world from this very website, FatCityEntertainment.com.

We already have people purchasing our games and hosting Fat Bottom Trivia or Music dobOFF in countries around the world, and I couldn’t be more excited about it. I also can’t wait for the fun new games coming out this Season!

Fat Bottom Trivia is a “Game Show” style event similar to Jeopardy!, but more interactive, with more side-games, media, and our funny Hosts love getting in close with audiences, and making sure it’s not just trivia, it’s an experience for everyone. Music dobOFF are download-and-print games you can play with all audiences while listening to fun music from all eras. Now with over 30 different games from Zoo Rock to Food Fight!

At Fat City Entertainment, we don’t really have employees, we have Hosts. Fat City Hosts are freelance entrepreneurs, just like me. So if you are interested in doing more than just playing fun games, go ahead and make some money out there. Why not, we do! It’s fun, and it works! People can; and do, use our games to make money. We know that. And we want to help you do it too. So, if you are interested in a “career path” let’s call it. The first thing to do is download our e-book on How To Start A Successful Trivia Or Bar Games Night. And if you send me a personal email requesting it. I will send you a code to download it for free. Hit me up -> (dustin@fatcityentertainment.com)

The most important thing, though, is that the show goes on. That we are supporting our local communities though live entertainment. If I get busy (which I do), or if I don’t answer my email fast enough (which I don’t), one of our trusted Regional Managers will be happy to help out, and get you to the right solutions either through their experiences as Hosts, or in one of our many online forums. If you’re wondering how the Regional Managers got where they are, it started with them creating their very first show.

Yours truly,

Dusty Ramsbottom,
Entertainer & Founder, Fat City Entertainment
