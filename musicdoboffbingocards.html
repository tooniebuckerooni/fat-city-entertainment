How To Become Your Own Party's Entertainer

Becoming an entertainer doesn't have to be hard, we can help you out!​ Not only can you save some money but you can also put yourself in a position of authority and guidance for your party. Here's how to do it:

Choose your game

There's an infinite amount of games you can choose from. However we'll focus on Music Bingo and a "Game Show" style trivia. These two are the ones we've found to be the best suited for any audience. You can easily adapt them to fit your audience and they're a lot of fun!

​Here's what you need for both.

Everything You Need To Host Music Bingo

Music bingo can be incredibly easy and incredibly fun! All you need is to choose your theme and print your cards. Choosing your theme will depend on your audience. Do they love cinema? Go with our Movie Soundtrack set! Millenials will definitely love our 90s Bingo Game. What about hosting a party for a particular holiday? Halloween, Christmas, Mother's Day... there's a game for every event and every audience.

Once you have chosen your theme you'll need to print out the cards attached to it. They're all randomized so you won't get a single card that's the same as another one. Print them on bright-colored paper sheets for better results!

At the party all you need to do is to open the curated playlist on your favorite music app (Spotify, Amazon or Apple Music), set it in shuffle and start your Bingo game! If you want to add a bit more professionalism and realism use bingo daubers instead of regular pens for a more authentic experience.

Make sure to make your transitions to more difficult objectives in the correct order, and within an appropriate number of song clues (example: 4 corners should not be done before 2 lines because it is of similar difficulty, and you may already have a winner there.)

Choose how people can win:
  1. Single Line
  2. 2 Lines
  3. 4 corners
  4. An “ex” or a “plus sign”
  5. A “hashtag” (pound sign)
  6. Any of the letters in the word BEER (must go all the way to the sides, also a funny joke if there are children in the audience, is they have to do any of the letters in MILK)
  7.  Blackout

Or Try something new!

You can also make your own game and print your cards with our Music Bingo Card Generator!

Remember: as the host you're the heart and soul of the game. Joke with guests as often as possible. Have fun with them. Get the crowd laughing or dancing and you win the night!
