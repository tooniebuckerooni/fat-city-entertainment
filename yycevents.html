Trusted mobile event entertainers, or "Hosts", will travel to you on your event date. A few hours before guests arrive, they will set up full Audio/Visual gear and do a sound check.

We'll supply appropriate background music while your guests arrive, and we can DJ your cocktail hour or dinner, upon request. If you would like to give speeches, feel free to utilize out audio gear!

Choose Your Style Of Trivia "Game Show"

"Christmas" Themed Games Available For - Rock Jeopardy, Music Bingo, and Fat Bottom Trivia.

Rock Jeopardy!

A simple yet perfect classic.

Get your guests excited for a traditional trivia big-screen game show all about musical artists. Wholesome fun will get the party started. 6 Categories, and Final Jeopardy. Images and Video clues in certain categories. 

​​(Christmas themed categories available.)

Music Bingo

Very popular option this year. Everyone gets a "Music Bingo Card" and 'dob' off the songs as they are played over the speakers. Go for 1st line, 4 corners, or a Blackout for prizes.
Over 40 genre options to choose from. 

Note: This is a more casual option that permits your guest to socialize more during the games. They can sing and dance, if they choose. Or.. ask our Hosts for hints to help mark off their cards and make a Bingo! 

Fat Bottom Trivia

Our own version of Big-Screen Game Show Trivia, with Extra hidden Side-Games and Interactive Final Challenge created by our trivia writers perfect for team trivia fun times at holiday party just like yours.

This selection will  get your guests on their feet, singing, dancing, and making lasting memories. Choose from: General Knowledge, Music or Sports genres. 

(Most interactive and engaging option)

"Survey Style" Trivia Options:

"Friendly" Feud
Challenge your teams head-to-head with fun questions based on popular responses. Each round takes about 10-15 minutes, so it's best to discuss with our Fat City Hosts whether this is ideal for your time restraints and party size. 
Triv101
Our very own "Survey-Style" creation. Each team starts with 101 points and must work their way down to exactly zero by guessing the most popular responses to generalized questions.
