19 Music Bingo Games Our Crowds Can't Get Enough Of

4/11/2018

2 Comments

 
Music bingo unites the music lovers and trivia buffs in one fun event, and our bingo hosts know how to keep the crowd entertained with tricky cards and amazing playlists. Check out this list of our crowd favorites!

via GIPHY


It is hard to pick our favorite music bingo playlist because they are all so much fun, but these nineteen bingo games seem to be a sure bet with the crowds. Go ahead, ask one of our music bingo hosts or our loyal game players! They'll tell you these games make for the best music bingo shows anywhere!


For The Older Crowd

  • Life's A Beach: Those California girls down in Margaritaville drinking the tequila with a lime in a coconut, am I right? Although not everything in this category is from way back when, it has enough golden oldies to keep the retired crew digging the vibe with artists like The Beach Boys, Jimmy Buffet, and Don Henley on the list.
  • Golden Oldies: Speaking of golden oldies...yep, we've got a whole list dedicated to the classics. Bring on the classic entertainers like Elvis, Otis Redding, and good ol' Patsy Cline.
  • The 70's: A tumultuous decade that inspired artists to create some timeless tunes. This playlist includes songs from Joni Mitchell, Queen, and Lynyrd Skynyrd.
  • The Beatles: A band so iconic they get a category unto themselves. No one will be able to resist singing along when you play the Fab Four's classic hits like Yellow Submarine, Lucy in the Sky with Diamonds, and Blackbird.

Family Friendly Shows

  • Movie Soundtracks: These movie tunes span the decades which makes this the perfect game to play with a family crowd. Everyone knows which movie My Heart Will Go On comes from!
  • Disney: Got a lot of kiddos in the crowd? We've got a playlist for that too! Get the room excited when you hand out bingo cards full of Disney classics like Circle of Life, Under the Sea, and yes, even Let it Go!
  • TV Shows: Theme songs can be awesome (Fresh Prince of Bel Air), catchy (Scooby Doo), or down right iconic (Game of Thrones), but no matter the category, this game is perfect for the family team. It has a little something for each age group!
  • X-mas: Hey, everyone loves a good Christmas carol even in July. A holiday themed music bingo game with all your favorite tunes sang by all your favorite artists.

The Mixed Bag

  • Food Fight: At a new venue and you aren't sure about the vibe yet? Try a mixed, theme game! Different songs from different periods and genres, but they all have food in common! From a classic like Blueberry Hill to some gangsta rap by Snoop Dogg, everyone can play along.
  • Girls vs Boys: We got songs about girls like Girls Just Wanna Have Fun, and we got songs about boys like Boyz-n-the-Hood. Who will reign supreme?
  • Out of This World: Space the final frontier...of music bingo! We've got Space Cowboys, Star Wars, and Drops of Jupiter up in this theme. Far out, man.
  • One Hit Wonders: A classic music trivia category! Everyone knows these mega-hits, but do they know who sang them? Here's a freebie Tainted Love was a hit by a band called Soft Cell. You're welcome.

A Decade of Hits

  • And Then There Was Hair: Who doesn't love an old-fashioned rock ballad? The hair bands ruled the 80's, and we have an entire bingo card dedicated to those luscious locks. Poison, KISS, and Guns-n-Roses all have a square here.
  • Oh...the 90's: Was grunge the death of hair? The eternal debate! We aren't here to decide it though, we are just here to deliver these classic 90's hits from artists like Gin Blossoms, Beck, and No Doubt!
  • 2009 Billboard: Kanye's gonna let you finish, but first...here are the hits of the 09's! We've got Gaga, we've got Swift, and oh yes, we've got the man himself!

The Tough Stuff

  • Nicknames: Do you know the King of Pop? How about the Queen of Soul? Who is known as The Boss? This game isn't for the faint of heart! It can be a tough round, but the trivia fans will eat it up.
  • Countries: How many people know where their favorite artist hails from? This card names the country, you need to figure out the artist and where they come from. If we play Blissing Me, for instance, the player has to know the artist is Bjork AND know that she comes from Iceland. Not so easy is it?
  • Antonyms: Your clever players will love this one! The card may say something like "Stop You Down" which is the correct clue for Rolling Stones' Start Me Up! Can you figure them all out?
  • Anagrams: This is hands down our hardest game, and it is only recommended for your serious players as casual ones tend to get a bit frustrated. The answers are jumbled on the card into different words like Pearl Jam turns into Maple Jar. Whew. How'd we come up with this one?

There you have it folks! A collection of our music bingo crowd favorites in a tidy list just for you. Now to pick which game to play...

Host Your Own Show With A Music Bingo Membership!

If you have been hanging around these pages for awhile, then you are probably aware about our love for all thing music bingo! If you haven’t been here before, then let us enlighten you! Music Bingo is basically THE CHOICE for entertainment in pubs and restaurants across the country. Game play is pretty similar to regular bingo, except players have to match up the song being played to one of the clues on their card. It is one part bingo, one part dance party, and two parts music trivia!

Sounds like a blast, right?

Trust us, it is. Here at Fat City Entertainment, we already host two weekly music bingo shows, and we are always answering questions from people wanting to host their own show.  So, because of all the buzz, we decided to create a Music Bingo Membership! Our membership will give you all the supplies you need to start your own show, no matter where you live. The bingo cards are available for instant download, and the bingo daubers are shipped right to your door. Boom. Music Bingo Show.


First of all, let’s talk about those cards. Your membership will include enough cards to play twelve different games. This variety will make it easy for you to set up a weekly or monthly show, repeat shows are the best way to keep the revenue flowing! The subjects and music genres vary so you can play to your crowd, whether young or old, with things like The Beatles, Hair Bands, Michael Jackson, and One Hit Wonders. Some of the cards are a little trickier than others so choose your games wisely! Worried about where you will find all that music? Don’t be! The cards come with an accompanying Spotify playlist to make it super easy for you to get started.

The rules of the game are super simple and easy to customize! Have your players get a tradition bingo (vertical, horizontal, or diagonal), or play games like 4 corners or double bingo. Have a playlist that you really love? Maximize the amount of songs you can hear by playing a full card blackout game!


Next let’s discuss your show! Music bingo is an easy show to host, much easier to get started than a trivia show or open mic night. A music bingo host can run a 2 hour show in just about any venue with minimal equipment to set up. Just bring along these bingo cards and daubers, the Spotify playlist, and an iPod or laptop. If you can plug into your venue’s sound system that is great, otherwise, you may need to bring your own speaker. We also suggest using a microphone for hosting, but it is not absolutely necessary especially if you know how to project your voice.

You can easily insert interactive bonus rounds or prizes in your game by making people sing or dance along to the music. Remember, you are the host! Make the show your own, and it will be that much more entertaining for your group!



Last, let’s get excited about the daubers! It may not seem like they are that big of a deal, and you can surely run your show with a box of crayons, but your players will find your show that much more memorable when you whip out these multi-colored daubers. It adds just that little bit of flair that pub game players love. It can give your show an edge over the competition, especially if you are competing with a lot of venues in your area.

Print your bingo sheets on colored paper to add even more fun, but just remember to make sure your print is legible and your daubers marks can be seen on whatever paper color you choose.

Up The Fun Ante with a Fat City Entertainment Music Bingo Night!

2/16/2018

0 Comments

 
The people have spoken, and they want bingo...music bingo that is. Yep. Just in case you haven’t heard, let us tell you: music bingo is fast becoming the entertainment of choice at pubs, bars, and restaurants, and it is getting people in the doors every day of the week!

What is Music Bingo?

Music bingo is a super fun, fast-paced take on the traditional bingo game! Instead of getting lame cards with a bunch of numbers on them, the players are given cards that contain song titles, artist names, genres, or other music-related items.

Then, the bingo host plays short 15-20 second snippets of songs, and the players must match up those songs to the items on their bingo cards! Sounds way more exciting then listening to someone call out B-32 and O-17 for hours, right?
How does Music Bingo work?

Your music bingo event will go off without a hitch when you have a Fat City Entertainment host on the scene! Our hosts arrive at your venue with all the necessary tools to make your bingo night run smoothly, They bring the bingo cards, daubers, speakers, music, and fun.

The shows run for 2-3 hours with each round lasting about 30 minutes. To keep it fresh, the rules of each round vary, and can include traditional bingo games like four corners or less-traditional things like anagrams!

The host also changes up the playlists to keep the crowd from getting bored with the same old pop hits. Bingo songs can come from all music genres! We also like to throw a few curve balls with lists like Disney classics, Artists named Will, or famous trios.
