“The Fab Four” Music Bingo Cards FREE Download

1/12/2020

0 Comments

 
 Published: Jan 12, 2020, Written by: Cornel Manu

All Beatles music lovers unite!

We have a new collection that you can use for your music bingo parties. Do you think you have what it
takes to recognize any Beatles song? Then this game is for you.

We created for you “The Fab Four” Music dobOFF Bingo cards that you can print and use to host your
own Music Bingo events. We offer you 60 randomized Bingo Cards with Free Space.

The playlist that you need to use contains 30 Beatles songs. We recommend playing 60-90 seconds of
each song and use the playlist on shuffle. Playing the full songs can make the guests bored and the event
longer. Go for a Line, 4 Corners, or a Blackout!

We always recommend handing out Bingo Dobbers such as these ones:
https://www.amazon.com/DAB-INK-3oz-Bingo-Daubers/dp/B01C4QBW3G.

Printing on bright colored paper is always a bonus, as it makes the game more appealing and fun. I think
the 60lb cardstock may be the best:
https://www.amazon.com/Neenah-80944-01-Astrobrights-Colored-Cardstock/dp/B01LX0UJBN.

The Bingo Cards are in PDF format, and each page is set to “Landscape” mode. That means you print 2
bingo cards on each sheet of paper. You can cut the bingo cards with a scissor, or you can give your
guests 2 bingo cards at a time.

If you need more than 60 Bingo Cards for a bigger audience, simply ask us and we will give them to you.
This game was specially created for our elite members.

Playing this free music bingo game is sure to get everyone excited. This might not be pleasant for young
audiences that might not recognize any of the Beatles songs, but you can be sure that will be a hit for
those that grew up listening to "Here Comes The Sun" and "All You Need Is Love".

Bring along some Beatles themes prizes for the winners, and you will have a blast. For example, you can
buy The Beatles - Memorabilia Pack from Amazon, which includes four booklets stuffed with photos and stats for each Beatle; Fan Club paperwork, concert handbill, 1963 programme; Cavern Club booklet, mixed ephemera, plus a collection of cards. This is a replica gift that any Beatles fan would appreciate.

Use this link to download the “The Fab Four” Bingo Cards:
https://www.fatcityentertainment.com/store/p134/thefabfour.html

And these are the links to the playlists that you need to use for the Music Bingo:
Spotify - Apple

Consider serving foods that are mentioned in Beatles songs to add some extra atmosphere. That means
you can serve truffles, cornflakes, marshmallows, eggs, strawberries, pies, honey and turkey. If you are feeling extra special, you can serve octopus as well. We aren’t chefs, so take this advice with a grain of
salt.

Have fun!

The Easiest Way To Start A Music Bingo Trivia Night In 2020

1/9/2020

0 Comments

 

Congratulations on surviving another year.

I hope you all had fantastic holidays!

There is a lot on the docket for 2020, so let's jump right in!

Let's talk about the hot Music Bingo Games for 2020.

We now have over 30 music bingo games available in the Trivia Store.

The best deal per game and top option for recurring trivia shows is still the Music dobOFF Gold Pack.

However, you will notice the total numbers of games included has jumped up to 33 games at once!

The Silver Pack is also updated to include a compilation of the Top 20 games played from 2019.

(The Bronze Pack hasn't changed, and still contains only the Top 10 music bingo games.)

The biggest innovation of the year will most definitely be...

...hosting trivia nights in-house. For most small businesses, hiring entertainers (like me) is not feasible on a regular basis. Hey, I still suggest ringing me up before the holidays and discussing a potential show, sometimes I just want to try something new, and see how it goes. Probably for a heavy discount. This is how the Bold Missy Brewery Drag Queen Game Show Night arose (so much fun) among many others.

So, the answer is simple. Download the same games I use at my events, and host it yourself. That's why we made the games the way we have! They can be easily reproduced and customized for your event.

Each pack of music bingo cards come in a .pdf file that you can print off as often as you choose, and however many cards you require for the event. The default number of cards in a pack is 60. However, with you purchase, you can request however many cards you need generated. We'll get back to you during regular business hours with a fresh file to download with exactly the number you need.

You'll also find pre-made playlists on Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon Prime Music.

Music Bingo is a good option for those that don't want to connect a Big Screen. Print off as many cards as you require, and play music from the accompanying playlist. You can always purchase Bingo Daubers, as well, or just use markers.

Try giving out prizes. I am not a fan of cheap plastic gifts, that are little more than garbage. But a few nicer gifts, or gift certificates to your establishments are always good options.
