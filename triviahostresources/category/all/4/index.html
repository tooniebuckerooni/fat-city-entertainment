What is Music Bingo?

Music bingo is a fun and interactive game that combines the excitement of bingo with the enjoyment of music. Instead of using traditional bingo numbers on the bingo cards, each square on the card contains the name of a popular song, artist, or music genre. A bingo caller plays short clips of songs, and players mark off the corresponding squares on their cards.
The first player to complete a specific pattern on their bingo card, such as a straight line, diagonal, or full house, calls out "bingo" and wins a prize.
