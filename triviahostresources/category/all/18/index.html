Setting Up Sound Equipment, A Video Tutorial

7/4/2018

0 Comments

 
If you are new to trivia hosting or music bingo hosting, you may be wondering about the technical aspects of things. Well, Fat City is here to help! In our first video, we showed you how to print quality music bingo cards at home, and now, we want to show you how to set up all that sound equipment. It can seem a lot trickier than it is, but this video tutorial will have you looking like a pro in no time.

There is a lot of equipment that goes into making a Fat City host's toolkit, and although some of it is based on personal preference, most of the items mentioned in this video are what a host needs to run a show with good sound quality. 
​
Here's a list of all the equipment Dustin mentions in the video:
  • Amplifier
  • Speaker
  • Speaker Stand
  • Speaker Wire (male to male)
  • Roller Stand
  • Mic Stand
  • Microphone
  • Microphone Wire (XLR male to female)
  • Power Cord for Amplifier
  • Power Bar
  • Dance Light
  • Laptop or Ipad
  • Aux Cord 
  • Buzzers
  • Masking Tape

What TV Shows, Netflix Originals, and Movies Will You Be Watching in July?

6/21/2018

0 Comments

 
Here's our picks for the best of the best for streaming this summer!
Netflix has announced what is coming and going for the month of July, and we wanted to put together a little list of the streaming options that should not be missed. With all of the new series, movies, and Netflix original content, it can be a little difficult to pick your entertainment when you finally get around to all that Netflix chilling. Here is our quick little guide to take the pressure of decision making off of you!
​

6 Things to Watch on Netflix in July
  • Interview with a Vampire: This Anne Rice classic starring Tom Cruise, Brad Pitt, Antonio Banderas, and a tiny Kirsten Dunst is a must see for anyone that is a fan of the vampire genre. The costumes are fantastic, and Pitt turns in a perfectly angsty performance. However, Dunst is really the powerhouse of the film stealing every scene that she is in. 
  • How It Ends: A post-apocalyptic thriller about a man traveling thousands of miles to find his pregnant wife. This Netflix original film stars Theo James of the Divergent series, Kat Graham, and Forest Whitaker. 
  • Legacy of a Whitetail Deer Hunter: A comedy film about a deer hunter trying to reconnect with his son over a weekend hunting trip. With a cast that includes the hilarious Danny McBride and Thanos himself, Josh Brolin, this movie is a sure bet for an evening of laughs.
  • Sacred Games (Season 1): An original Indian Netflix production, the first season of Sacred Games includes 8 1-hour episodes. The plot involves a Mumbai cop and the machinations of a crime lord, the series is an adaptation of a crime novel by the same name. 
  • Orange is the New Black (Season 6): After season five's cliff-hanger, fans have been anxiously awaiting the new OITNB season! Netflix has signed the show for seasons 6 and 7, however, there have been rumors that it may wrap up in season 6...
  • Shameless (Season 8): The Gallagher family is back on Netflix for another 12 episodes, so get your binge-watching chair ready! This Showtime show has a bit of a cult following, so if you haven't yet been introduced, you have a lot of catching up to do! 

Alright, so that is most of the summer planned. Looks like July is going to be spent on the couch!

What will you be watching this summer?

Fat Bottom Trivia's Newest Interactive Game is Ready for Download: Episode 4.2

0 Comments

 
Download the newest set of categories in the trivia store!

If you are a trivia host, aspiring game show host, or just a really big fan of trivia in general, then do we have a treat for you. The newest episode of our Fat Bottom Trivia interactive games is ready for download because we are officially launching Season 4 Episode 2. Hosts we have done all the work for you and put together an excellent interactive game show that is sure to be a winner with your trivia players.


General Knowledge! Side Games! Wager Round! Oh, my!
Episode two contains general knowledge questions with a moderate difficulty level that should be suitable for adult trivia crowds. It contains five amazing categories including Arts & Culture, The Hibbity Dibbity (yep, this category is all about drugs), and Yearbook Photos (laugh at celebrity high school photos).

It also includes three hidden side games to get your crowd out of their chairs and on their feet. These side games are physical and interactive. Crowd participation for the side games is usually the most memorable part of our live shows, and one of the main reasons our hosts and players can't get enough Fat Bottom Trivia!

Our Dare You 2 Wager Round is a bit like Final Jeopardy. Each of the teams gets the chance to bet before answering the question, and often times it completely changes the team rankings. Brave teams can bet it all or teams can choose to play it safe, however the betting falls, it is sure to add that competitive edge your players will love.

Episode 4.2 is available today, and don't forget to grab episode 4.1 if you haven't already. Stay tuned because episode 4.3 will be here before you know it!
