Everyone loves a good game of music bingo, right? That must be why it is one of the fastest growing entertainment choices of bars and restaurants It is super easy to get started, and everyone loves a good playlist!

For those who have never played music bingo before, let me give you a quick rundown on how the game is played. Players are given bingo cards, but unlike traditional bingo cards that contain nothing but numbers, the squares on music bingo cards are filled with song titles or the names of music artists. The music bingo host plays a couple minutes of a song, and the players have to match the song played with one of the squares on their cards.

Does it sound like fun? It is, trust us. Plus, it can be super challenging! Many of the cards we use in our games aren’t all that straightforward, and the clues can be a bit tricky. Plus, we just love to play themed games like our newest set: Zoo Rock.

The Zoo Rock includes all your favorite hits with animals in the title, and there are more songs featuring animals than you probably knew existed! The Zoo Rock playlist includes songs like the Foo Fighter’s Monkey Wrench or Ginuwine’s Pony. See where this is going now?

These music bingo cards are available for instant download in the trivia store, and you can follow our playlist on Spotify. Easy peasy. You can be hosting your Zoo Rock show tonight! What are you waiting for?

We debuted the Zoo Rock cards at a show this past week, and it was a big hit!
