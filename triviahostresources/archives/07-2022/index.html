July The 4th Be With You! That's the Star Wars thing, right?

7/4/2022

0 Comments

 
I'm kidding, of course. Don't cancel me. I came out of my hole to share a great offer for everyone for a, hopefully, happy Summer this bright and shiny... and rainy and windy 2022!

I know it's the 4th of July and that's a great day to celebrate across the USA, but our games now travel even further than that, so I want to recognize the Music Bingo Enthusiasts in Canada, England, Ireland, Australia, and now even Turkey! Holy moly, word is getting out how cool this game is. If you're reading this and have known about Fat City games for some time now, then congratulate yourselves for helping to pioneer the movement back into social venues, and our shared passion for live entertainment.

There are a lot of things to stress about in the world today, but downloading and printing fun inexpensive Music Bingo games should not be one of them.

Have you been hosting Music Bingo events already? We want to hear about it!

Please share in the comment section below, or email us directly at [email protected]. 

Some common questions we want to ask you about you and your shows are:
  1. What kind of venue do you perform at?
  2. What is the age range or demographic of your audience?
  3. How long does your show usually run for?
  4. Do you give away prizes?
  5. How long from each song do you like to play? Are you a fast DJ, or does your crowd typically take their time to dance and sing?

Anything unique or something else that you think we would be interested in hearing about, please let us know. Let's get this discussion going, and better yet, convince those around us that fun live entertainment events like this are therapeutic, relaxing and break barriers. From one table to the next, our main goal is to get people out of their comfort zone, right? Maybe the dance a little, or remember an old forgotten memory, or meet a new friend. 

Keep it up out there, Friends. I know you are doing a great job, and truly helping your community. 

Dusty Ramsbottom
