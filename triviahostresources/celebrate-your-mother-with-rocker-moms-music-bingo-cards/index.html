Celebrate Your Mother With Rocker Moms Music Bingo Cards

5/16/2018

0 Comments

 
Say happy Mother's Day with a little music bingo celebration.
​

via GIPHY


The new Rocker Moms cards are now available for download or you can try to win a set in our giveaway, but hurry it won't last forever.
​
You know who loves a good game of music bingo? Your Mom! Well, she probably does considering it is one of the fastest growing entertainment choices of bars and restaurants across the country. Plus, It is super easy to get started, and everyone loves singing along to their favorite songs!

For those of you that have never played music bingo before, (uh, like where have you been? Seriously. It's everywhere) let me give you a quick rundown on how the game is played. Players are given printed cards to play, but unlike traditional bingo cards that contain nothing but numbers, the squares on music bingo cards are filled with song titles or the names of music artists. The music bingo host plays a couple minutes of a song, and the players have to match the song played with one of the squares on their cards. Easy peasy, right?

You guys know how much we love a good theme for our music bingo games, right? Well, we've done it again! Get excited for our newest game: Rocker Moms!

Rocker Moms includes all of your favorite hits that have something to do with moms! Some of them might be more obvious like the Mamas & the Papas, but some of them are a little tougher like music from How I Met Your Mother. Either way, your players will have a blast playing along with these mom songs.

Show your mom some appreciation on Mother's Day and all year round with the Rocker Moms music bingo cards!

​the Rocker Moms cards before time runs out!
