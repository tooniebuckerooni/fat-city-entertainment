How To Create An Awesome Music Bingo Party Event In 3 Easy Steps

6/28/2020

28 Comments

 
Here is complete instructions in how to create your own music trivia DJ night. 

It's super easy to create and host for friends, family, co-workers, or even great for a bar event!

The great thing about a music bingo night is it can be casual or interactive, depending on how you want to host it. 

And it is beyond easy to create. I'll tell you how!

Step 1: Print Off Bingo Sheets

Ok, maybe I got ahead of myself when I said it was free. But it's not far off. The first tiny expense is paper. I walked down to the UPS store and printed off 200 sheets (100 pages) for about ten bucks. 

If you have your own printer, you are covered there. 

​Now, you can either use my sheets that I have made right here if you are beyond lazy..
Now Over 30 Different Games To Choose From.

Or be proactive and use our new card generator.

Step 2: DJ Music Playlist On Spotify On Shuffle

This is my favorite part. So easy, so simple, so good...

If you are using our sheets, you can simple play this playlist!

It's on shuffle so you don't even need a spotify subscription!

Am I the best or what??​

Step 3: Pens are cheap, but 'dobbers' are better!

There is no doubt, that having something fun in your hand to play with makes everyone excited. Bingo Dobbers (sometimes spelled daubers) are an easy way to improve the quality of your show.

I highly recommend adding Bingo Dobbers to your events. This is the same thing as printing on coloured paper. Sure, you can simply use regular white paper. The game will still function the exact same. Of course, it will be better for your guests, and maybe you get more repeat customers, if you take the time to buy a stack of coloured paper first.

Try a thicker stock of paper, as well. 60lb paper stock works great.

That's it. Easy. Fun. Interactive Entertainment.
