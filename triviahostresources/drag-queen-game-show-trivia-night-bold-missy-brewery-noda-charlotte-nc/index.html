What To Expect On Your First Visit:

Fat Bottom Trivia "Game Show Nights" are a new & fun way to celebrate holidays, meet new people, impress your visitors, or staff team-building exercise.

We begin with General knowledge trivia and end with a Music Trivia Party

Using big-screens, buzzers, and interactive trivia, our hosts get teams laughing, singing, and buzzing in!

Check the calendar for holidays and opportunities coming up. and contact us before we book up. 

We have games ready for Halloween, Christmas, Birthdays, stags, and much more.
